# Kong

Kong is a web app that helps teams rule their workflow. It is built with a Supabase backend.

## Brand
- Main domain: `kong.com` (or your chosen URL)
- Hook: `Kong: Rule Your Workflow.`
- Nav: `Apex` | `Echo` | `Pulse`

## Product Lineup
- `Apex` (Projects): Claim your territory.
- `Echo` (Chat): Connect your troop.
- `Pulse` (Reviews): Track the heartbeat.

## Feature Pillars
### Apex (Project Management)
The vibe: high-altitude, total control, "top of the mountain."
The function: the command center where project leads see the full territory and drive execution.
Action copy: use `Launch Expedition` or `Claim Territory` instead of "Create Project."

### Echo (Chat)
The vibe: instant, wide-reaching, and resonant.
The function: fast, far-reaching communication for teams and groups.
Action copy: use `Send Echo` or `Broadcast` instead of "Send Message."

#### Echo Labels
- Troops: team folders or group chats
- Vibrations: notifications or active chats
- The Wild: discovery page for public groups

### Pulse (Reviews / Feed)
The vibe: real-time, social, and essential.
Why it works: it feels like a living community feed with continuous updates.
Action: users check Pulse to see what's trending.

## Roles & Controls
### Alpha (Owner / Admin)
- Apex: Overwatch view of all territories and role assignment.
- Apex: only an Alpha can reach the Summit (final approval stage).
- Echo: owns troops, can Silence or Broadcast to all members.
- Echo: use a distinct badge (Silver Crown or Golden Gorilla silhouette).
- Pulse: Alpha Verdicts can be pinned to the top of the feed.
- Pulse: moderation is called Alpha Intervention.

### Beta (Manager / Power User)
High permissions with reporting to the Alpha.

### Troop (General User)
Contributes to Echo and Pulse.
